Faster innovation in financial services ebook

Great to have you here on this page.

If you are in the financial services industry, or a provider to that industry, you will know that both innovation and regulation are pretty intense. (You may be in a different but similar industry, yet this might apply to you too).

I am writing an e-book on “Faster innovation in Financial Services”. This will focus on the skills that help speed up secure cloud adoption. The idea is that cloud computing represents an innovation eco-system. This includes FinTech companies and cloud providers. In that eco-system secure cloud adoption is the pathway to faster innovation.

I have based it on a number of interviews with senior people in the industry, as well as some desk research I have done.

Earlier, I wrote some blog posts on the basis of preliminary research. They include the following:

  • Banks love to take risks, or not?
  • What if the auditor is scared? (of cloud)
  • Skills for faster innovation in financial services.
  • Why lawyers need to understand some tech.

Do you want a pre-release of the ebook when it is ready? If you sign up to my updates I will send it to you.
